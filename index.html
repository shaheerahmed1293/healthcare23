HEALTHCARE

Lean, collaborative building for innovative, compassionate care

Success in healthcare construction depends on the right mix of trusted specialists, crystal-clear communication and team alignment on project goals. That’s how DPR approaches all of our healthcare projects. We devote the proper time and attention to assembling project teams tailored to each healthcare customer’s individual needs.

HEALTHCARE

Experts

DPR's team of healthcare experts combines the facility knowledge of a consultant with experience in virtual design and construction, prefabrication, technology, phasing, logistics, and equipment.

We are experienced builders with a keen understanding of the unique needs and applications of the intricate systems that are the lifeline of healthcare facilities.
